Our Story

Jed and Prescilla met in 2007 at transfer student orientation at the University of Puget Sound in Tacoma, Washington, "the City of Destiny." Both had transferred from the same school for the same reasons, and when the university president told Prescilla there was another transfer student from the same school she made it known that she was not interested in meeting that person.

A few hours later they bumped into each other and started talking. Jed was Prescilla's first friend at UPS, and Prescilla Jed's. They explored the town together, got to know each other, and in a few short months, fell in love.

It was also during university that they fell in love with travel. In the summer of 2009 Prescilla studied abroad in Salamanca, Spain, and convinced Jed that spending his summer research award on travel would be worth it. While reluctant at first, Jed got on a plane and promptly got lost trying to find his way out of the Madrid airport. But ultimately, they made it worth it by packing 8 countries into three weeks' travel. The pace of the trip definitely tested and strengthened their young relationship and kindled their love for experiencing new places.

Since then Jed and Prescilla have been together, traveling at every opportunity. When Jed made a one-year commitment to teach English in Korea in 2012, they made it work long-distance and used the opportunity for an extended trip through Southeast Asia.

Currently they live in Portland, and are happy to be planning not only their next trip but the rest of their lives together. We are very excited and honored to have you join us as we set out on our married lives together.

The Proposal

On 4 July 2015 Jed proposed to Prescilla on the Puget Sound in Seattle's Discovery Park. She said yes!
